Information About Weight Lifting

Bench Press:

The bench press is a popular exercise for strengthening the chest, shoulders, and triceps. It involves lying on a bench and pushing a barbell away from the chest.

Dead lift:

The deadlift is a compound exercise that works the muscles in the lower back, glutes, hamstrings, and calves. It involves lifting a barbell from the floor to a standing position.

Squat:

The squat is a compound exercise that targets the muscles in the legs, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, and calves. It involves bending the knees and lowering the body while keeping the back straight.
